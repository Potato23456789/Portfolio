  • Plant Generator — parametric foliage with growth controls
  • Tree Branch — recursive branching system
  • Flower — petal instancing on curves
  • Christmas Tree Ornament — parametric decoration

Simulation & Dynamics

  • Particle Simulation with Lifetime — birth/death cycle & forces
  • Particle Trail — motion trail effects
  • Ocean Wave — displacement-based wave animation
  • IcoSphere Organism — organic growth animation

Architectural & Product Tools

  • Wall Pieces — modular wall assembly system

Animation Techniques
